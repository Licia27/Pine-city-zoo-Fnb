TIGERS

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family and are renowned for their power and striking orange-and-black striped coats. Each tiger's stripe pattern is unique, providing camouflage in their natural habitats.

Native to Asia, tigers are solitary hunters and can be found in a variety of environments, including forests, grasslands, and swamps. They are carnivores, preying on deer, wild boar, and other large mammals.

Tigers are excellent swimmers and often cool off in pools or streams. Sadly, tigers are endangered due to habitat loss and poaching, making conservation efforts critical for their survival.
