ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Whether you choose to walk or ride, you will find the monkey trail very challenging. Take the 30 minute or 1 hour trail.

Monkey Trail is a fun and exciting way to explore the zoo. You can see monkeys, lemurs, and other primates up close as you walk or ride through the trail.

Be prepared to get dirty!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Monkey Trail is at the eastern side of the zoo.
