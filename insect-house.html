INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is home to one of the largest and most diverse insect collections in the world. Here you will see creepy crawlies from all over the world, in every shape and size.

Insect House is home to a variety of insects, including butterflies, beetles, and ants. You can also see live insect displays, where you can learn about their life cycles and habitats.

Insect House is a great place to learn about the importance of insects in our ecosystem. Insects play a vital role in pollination, decomposition, and food chains.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Insect House is at the eastern side of the zoo, next to the Monkey Trail.
