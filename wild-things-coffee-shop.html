WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

Come and relax with a fragrant, freshly-brewed cuppa at Wild Things Coffee Shop. We serve a variety of coffees, teas, and delicious desserts.

They also serve a variety of sandwiches, confectionery and desserts, in case you're peckish!

Wild Things Coffee Shop is a great place to take a break and enjoy the beautiful surroundings of the zoo. You can sit outside on the patio and watch the animals while you sip your drink.

Wild Things Coffee Shop is also a great place to meet friends and family. You can enjoy a meal together and catch up on the latest news.
