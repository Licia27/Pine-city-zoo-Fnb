THE LOST FOREST

Relax and enjoy a picnic with your family and friends in this shady picnic spot by the edge of the lake.

The Lost Forest is a beautiful and serene place to relax and unwind. You can enjoy the sounds of nature and the gentle breeze as you sit by the lake.

There are plenty of shady spots to sit and enjoy a picnic, and you can also take a stroll around the lake to see the beautiful scenery.

Picnic tables and clean bathrooms are provided.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest to the east of the zoo, beyond Monkey Trail.
